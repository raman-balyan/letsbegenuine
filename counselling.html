Embracing The Wholeness Of Life!

Relaxing on the rooftop on a Monday morning...

Indulging in your favorite hobby everyday...

Frequent family time together at home...

Travelling with your spouse every month...

Enjoying working remotely by the mountain side...

What if life could include all of this without having our jobs control us?

How would it be if Monday mornings found us with the same enthusiasm as Friday evenings?

We at Letsbegenuine.com have been able to gradually see that work and life are not two isolated aspects of our lives. It is also no longer about striving to achieve a work life balance and never being able to get there. We believe it is about work life INTEGRATION and embracing life fully as one whole without fragmenting it. It’s about integrating minds, integrating ideas and integrating people.

Context

Organizations today find it challenging to sustain motivation amongst their top and middle leadership and the same therefore percolates down the line. Despite several training models, leadership theories and modern coaching methods, the results are only temporary. Several thought leaders in the field have challenged the existing organizational models and questioned the effectiveness of hierarchies.

We are NOT here to provide a permanent solution to this omnipresent, persistent issue either, because the solution lies within. True inspiration and motivation comes from a deep connection with the self and we facilitate the process of helping participants discover it for themselves. Our purpose is to ultimately eliminate the need for training by encouraging people to just be themselves at all times through fearless expression of the real self rather than just wearing the mask of a ‘trained professional’.

Our Approach


INTROSPECT

No real change is possible without deep introspection. Introspecting on where we currently are and what we would like to see for ourselves, is the first step.

CHALLENGE

Newness only comes when the old, stale and rigid are challenged. Our rigid belief systems are often the greatest barrier in our journey and challenging them is like opening the window of the intellect to let freshness come in.

INTEGRATE

Finally life is never about separation. It is never about welcoming pleasure and rejecting pain, it is never about work different from leisure; life always has both aspects to it. Accepting this and fully acknowledging life is the key to wholesome living.
